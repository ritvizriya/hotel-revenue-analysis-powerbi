# 🏨 Hotel Revenue Insights Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📊 Project Overview
This project analyzes hotel performance across multiple cities, room categories, and booking platforms to identify revenue opportunities and business inefficiencies.

---

## 🎯 Objective
To answer key business questions:
- Which city generates the most revenue?
- Are pricing strategies aligned with demand?
- How do booking channels impact profitability?
- Which segments drive the most revenue?

---

## 📌 Key Insights

- Total Revenue: ₹1.71 Billion (3 months)
- Mumbai contributes 39% of total revenue
- Delhi has highest occupancy (60.5%) but lowest revenue → pricing issue
- Weekends show higher demand, but ADR remains constant → missed opportunity
- 40% bookings come from third-party platforms; direct bookings only 5%
- Elite room category generates highest revenue (₹560M)

---

## 💡 Recommendations

- Implement dynamic pricing during peak demand
- Increase direct bookings to reduce commission dependency
- Improve low-rated properties to boost revenue
- Focus on high-performing segments like Elite rooms

---

- Business Analysis
